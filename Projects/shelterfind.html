ShelterFind

ShelterFind

Install on Playstore

ShelterFind is a mobile application that is meant to eliminate the hassle people go through to search for accommodation. It is primarily meant for two types of people: People who are looking for a place to stay (Guests) and those who are ready to offer their property for others (Hosts). These two types of users form a good pair of dynamic duos that actually need each other to operate well and complete. Hosts can provide information about their properties and facilities through the app. This information would act as the bases for the search results displayed to the user.

What shelterFind is offering

  • A high level of convenience when choosing a place. No need for travelling agencies, all you need is a few taps to make a decision.
  • Two sides get what they need with no hassle. A good deal can be made directly between a host and a guest within minutes in one platform.
  • Deal is directly betw1een hosts and guests, no middle men involved, hence low cost.
  • A wide variety of facilities to choose from, hence everybody finds a perfect place to fit.
  • People who have a free place they want to earn money with.

ShelterFind provides key functionality for both "Hosts" and "Guests" to make everybody satisfied. "Hosts" and "Guests" would have the following in common when using the app.

  • Registration or login using email or google
  • Adapter personal settings such as changing of name or password, uploading a profile picture.
  • Notification system
  • Userâ€™s dashboard

Apart from the list above, "Hosts" and "Guests" are provided with individual set of functionality. Anybody who signs up for ShelterFind is automatically a guest and a potential host. A guest becomes a host when he/she owns a facility in ShelterFind. In that case you are provided with additional responsibility and functionality. Individual responsibility of "Hosts" and "Guests" are listed below.

What guests would need when they visit the platform

  • Adding and changing information on their personal profiles
  • View details of any facility
  • The search option to find space or a facility
  • The filter option to search facilities based on what is available in the facility (example; amenities, bed type, price range
  • Rating facilities
  • Comment on facilities
  • Chat, communicate, call hosts
  • Add facilities to favorites
  • Share favourite facilities with friends or family

What Hosts would need when they visit the platform

  • Convenient navigation when changing anything in profile
  • Set options for users to see details on facility view page
  • Add new facilities and manage existing facilities
  • Ability to manage the data about facility; prices, photos, details
  • Communicating with guests

The following are limited to "Hosts" and "Guests"

Limitations to Guests

  • Cannot edit or modify someone's profile or facility

Limitations to Host

  • Cannot edit or modify someone's profile or facility
  • Cannot rate your own facility
  • Cannot comment your own facility
