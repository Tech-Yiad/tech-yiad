Make Path

How Long Can You Stack To Build a Path For the Ball

Brief Description

The main concept of the game is to keep a moving ball on pieces of cubes that are stacked to form a horizontal platform on which the ball moves. The ball is first positioned on a cube, and it begins to move forward on the cube. There is another cube in front of the existing one that moves sideways in two directions. You are supposed to tap on the moving cube at the right time so that it fits to the existing one, increasing the path or length of the platform of the moving ball. If the cube does not fit exactly on the existing cube, the part that do not match breaks off, reducing the width of the cube. You have to build a path as far as possible to keep the moving ball on. The game requires skill and concentration as you must find the right time to fit a cube on the previous one, but by every placement, the next on goes even faster and you still have to keep the ball on it. So much attention is needed. If you fit the cube too soon or too late on the previous one, the part that does not fit with the previous one is cut off giving you less space to fit the next cube. The ball falls off if the space becomes too small.

Features of the Game

A classic 3D timing game with cubes to fit and a ball to keep. All combined with innovative gameplay and smooth object interactions.
